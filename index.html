Moodle

Moodle is a group of board and video gamers. We primarily play casually, but sometimes we play competitively.

Our motto is "Be Nice." Essentially, we believe that having a good time is as simple as being civil.

Is Moodle dead?

Moodle members come and go with the flow of time, but the group is still alive and strong.

Many of us have reduced our online presence for various reasons, but there are still many who enjoy a good game of DoDS or TF2.

Contacting Us

To make our site easier to maintain, we decided to retire our blog and forums. They may make reappear in the future, but for now, we'll have to rely on other means of Moodle-related communication.

Connect to our Mumble server to say hi, or check out our Steam group.
